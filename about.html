What is Boss Meter?

Boss Meter is a fun, lightweight website designed for entertainment purposes. It generates a randomized "boss level" score meant to be playful and motivational, not a scientific or psychological measurement.

This site is an independent project created to explore simple web interactions, clean design, and engaging user experiences. No account is required, and no personal data is collected directly by the site.

All results generated by Boss Meter are for entertainment purposes only. They should not be interpreted as advice of any kind, including financial, personal, or professional decisions. Users are responsible for how they interpret and use the information shown on this site.

Enjoy responsibly and treat results as a fun moment, not a recommendation.
